What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Cookies We Use

We use the following cookies:

  • Session Cookie: A temporary cookie that helps maintain your browsing session. It is deleted when you close your browser.
  • Analytics Cookies: We collect anonymous data about page visits, including URL, referrer, screen size, and user agent information. This helps us understand website traffic and improve our services.

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect visitor data for:

  • Understanding website traffic patterns
  • Improving user experience
  • Enhancing our services

Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with third-party advertising or analytics services. All data is stored securely on our servers.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website features.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
